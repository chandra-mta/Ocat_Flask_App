Target Parameter Update Status Help Page

When you are ready to sign-off the box, just click the "Sign-off" button. Your name and the sign-off date will appear in that box.

If "Sign-off & Approve" button appears, by clicking it, you can sign it off, and also approve that obsid. You will receive a confirmation email of the submission.

If there are multiple revisions of the same obsid on the list:

  1. Different revisions of the same obsid are indicated by the same color marker on the "OBSID.revision" column.
  2. If "Multiple Revisions Open" is listed in the "Note column," the obsid has multiple revisions that are still open.
  3. If "Higher Rev # Was Already Signed Off" is listed in the "Note column," the obsid has a newer entry already signed off.
  4. You can clear all these "out-of-date" entries by clicking the "Close" button, even if general obscat, ACIS SI Mode, or HRC SI Mode edits columns are not signed off yet. Any of the unsigned column entries will be recorded as "N/A" in the database.
